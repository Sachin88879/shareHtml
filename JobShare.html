Job Description

We are seeking a skilled Blockchain Developer to join our dynamic team. As a Blockchain Developer, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and supporting blockchain-based applications. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features, while ensuring the integrity and performance of blockchain infrastructure.

Responsibilities:

  1. Design, develop, and maintain blockchain solutions.
  2. Collaborate with product managers and other stakeholders to define project requirements.
  3. Implement smart contracts and decentralized applications (DApps) on various blockchain platforms.
  4. Ensure the security, scalability, and efficiency of blockchain systems.
  5. Conduct code reviews, testing, and debugging to ensure high-quality software delivery.
  6. Stay updated on emerging blockchain technologies and industry trends.
  7. Provide technical guidance and support to team members.
  8. Troubleshoot and resolve blockchain-related issues.

Requirements:

  1. Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field. Master's degree preferred.
  2. Proven experience as a Blockchain Developer or similar role.
  3. Strong proficiency in blockchain concepts, protocols, and platforms (e.g., Ethereum, Hyperledger, Corda).
  4. Solid understanding of cryptography, consensus algorithms, and distributed systems.
  5. Proficiency in programming languages such as Solidity, Java, Python, or Go.
  6. Experience with smart contract development and deployment.
  7. Familiarity with blockchain development tools and frameworks (e.g., Truffle, Remix, Web3.js).
  8. Excellent problem-solving and communication skills.
  9. Ability to work independently and as part of a team in a fast-paced environment.
  10. Experience with DevOps practices and cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure) is a plus.
